About Us

Today time is a commodity which is under constant threat by distractions and an overwhelming amount of choices. By treating time as a finite and concrete resource that can be invested and spent we can be more mindful of how we use it. We are empowered to make choices with our time to achieve our goals and spend it on what matters most in life. By doing this we avoid feeling like time is just flying by without spending it on things we value and can enjoy life healthily and care-free knowing that there is not a better way we could be doing things as we have carefully planned our life by all the things we prioritize and attributed the time we see fit to achieve them be it - work, a side-hustle or project or family, friends and hobbies.

Our Services

Time Allocation and Management

Manage a "time account" by allocating time to upcoming deadlines, goals, or activities of personal value. Treat time as a finite resource to be invested wisely.

Goal-Oriented Planning and Tracking

Break down main goals into smaller, actionable tasks and assign realistic time periods to each. Use tools to track progress, visualize time spent on goals, and measure achievements versus pending tasks.

Dynamic and Interactive Scheduling

Utilize a dynamic, task-based calendar to organize and prioritize tasks in designated time slots. The calendar adapts to the user's schedule while maintaining alignment with their long-term goals.

Insights and Visualization for Better Time Use

Gain insights into time usage, including analysis of time wastage and its impact. Visualize progress, commitments, and time distribution to ensure a balanced work-life approach and alignment with priorities.
